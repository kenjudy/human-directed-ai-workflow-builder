**Task:** Preparing and sending a weekly status update to my manager and stakeholders

**Cycle frequency:** Weekly  

**Human owns:**

- Deciding how much detail on a sensitive risk to share with which audience
- Sending the update to external stakeholders

**AI executes (with review gates):**

- Drafts the metrics section from tracker data — human reviews before sending
- Drafts the risks list from open tickets — human reviews before sending
- Drafts a first-pass narrative summary — human reviews before sending

**Done looks like:**

- All shipped items reflected accurately; every risk above medium severity has a named owner; every ask names a person and a deadline; I read and edited the final draft myself  

**Common failure modes to prevent:**

- A real risk buried in the details; the update sent without my own read-through; stale data from last week's draft

**Phase boundaries:**

- Analysis done: tracker data pulled, open risks known
- Execution done: full draft complete, before my own read-through  

**STOP triggers (immediate human intervention):**

- Stop if AI drafts a risk statement about a stakeholder's team without my review
- Stop if AI sends or schedules the update without my explicit sign-off
- Stop if AI uses data older than this week without flagging it as stale

**Learning loop focus:**

- Track which flagged risks never actually mattered, to sharpen judgment on what's worth surfacing
